Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service.

We do not collect payment information or sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and display your personal expense records.
  • To generate summaries and breakdowns of your spending.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (DM Sans, DM Serif Display). Font files are loaded from Google's servers.

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request deletion of your data, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app.
